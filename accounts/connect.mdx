---
title: 'Connect'
description: 'A Proven Account is how Proven connects to all external vendors (ie CSP/Git/etc.)'
icon: 'link'
---

<Note> All accounts are at the Organizational Unit (OU) level. If you want the same account across different OU's, you must connect it in <Tooltip tip="Coming soon: A copy button, where you can select which OU to copy an account to!">each</Tooltip> OU.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    _Navigate_ to the [Proven Console](https://console.proven.cloud) > _Click_ your name in the top right > [Accounts](https://console.proven.cloud/accounts)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect">
    _Select_ **Connect Account** > _Input_ the required information > _Click_ **Connect Account**
    <Note>Each Account has vendor-specific required information. See them all [here](/accounts/types) </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate">
    _Verify_ status is **Connected** (Green dot) > If not, _select_ the Account > _Click_ **Validate Account**
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your account is now **Connected** and ready for use: [Create a Service](/services/create)
