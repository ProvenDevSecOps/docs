title: 'Update'

icon: 'wrench'
---

<Warning> All updates apply immediately!</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    _Navigate_ to the [Proven Console](https://console.proven.cloud) > _Click_ your name in the top right > [Accounts](https://console.proven.cloud/accounts)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update">
    _Select_ your **Account** > _Update_ the required information > _Click_ **Update Account**
    <Note>Each Account has vendor-specific required information. See them all [here](/accounts/all) </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your account is now **updated** and ready for use: [Create a Service](/services/create)
