---
title: 'Remove'
description: 'Remove your Account connection to Proven'
icon: 'link-slash'
---

<Note>This account cannot be used in a Proven Service: [Update a Proven Service](/services/update) to remove it before continuing</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    _Navigate_ to the [Proven Console](https://console.proven.cloud) > _Click_ your name in the top right > [Accounts](https://console.proven.cloud/accounts)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove">
    _Select_ your **Account** > _Click_ **Remove Account** > _Confirm_ the removal
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your account is now **removed**.
