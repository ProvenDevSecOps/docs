As a user you can set User Preferences for Default OU, Timezone, and Notifications:

- [Global](https://console.proven.cloud/user/preferences)
- [US](https://console.us.proven.cloud/user/preferences)
- [EU (Coming Soon)](https://console.eu.proven.cloud/user/preferences)


## Default OU
Your preference here determines what Org OU you sign in with. It can differ from the Default Org OU and you can update it to any OU you have permission to. This does not prevent you from switching your OU either.

<Note>You cannot set your Default OU to a cross-org OU at this time.</Note>

## Timezone
Your perference here determines the default [Canonical Timezone](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_tz_database_time_zones) you see in the Proven Console. You can click (or hover) displayed times to switch between your perference, UTC, and human readable.
